Car Maintenance Guide

Learn how to maintain your vehicle for better performance and longer lifespan.

Basic Maintenance Tips

  • Check tire pressure regularly
  • Change engine oil every 5,000–10,000 km
  • Inspect brakes for safety
  • Keep your battery in good condition

EV Maintenance

  • Monitor battery health
  • Update software regularly
  • Check charging system

Hybrid Maintenance

  • Maintain both engine and battery
  • Regular system diagnostics
