Overview

All car models introduced.

Electric Vehicles (EV)

Electric vehicles are powered entirely by electricity, offering zero emissions and lower running costs. They are ideal for city driving and environmentally conscious users seeking sustainable mobility.

Fuel Vehicles

Traditional fuel-powered vehicles remain widely used due to their accessibility and long driving range. They are suitable for long-distance travel and areas with limited charging infrastructure.

Hybrid Vehicles

Hybrid vehicles combine a fuel engine with an electric motor, improving fuel efficiency while reducing emissions. They provide a balanced solution for users transitioning toward greener transportation.

Plug-in Hybrid Vehicles (PHEV)

Plug-in hybrids offer both electric-only driving and fuel backup. They can be charged externally and are ideal for users who want flexibility between electric commuting and long-distance travel.
